Why MESCL

We are a leading supplier of Solar Cooking equipment in Uganda, located in Buyembe, Mayuge district. We’re a team of young enthusiasts who will stop at nothing to spread the word about using the direct energy of the sun to prepare the local meals we love.

We’ve built this with field work — demonstrations, home and group cooking parties, and steady growth across years of project implementation. If you’ve ever wanted a cleaner, smarter way to cook, you’re in the right place.

What we do

Integrated solar cooking appliances, energy-saving stoves, accessories, demos, and delivery.

Where we’re based

Buyembe, Mayuge District — serving nearby districts in Eastern Uganda.
