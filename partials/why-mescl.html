Why MESCL

We are the leading supplier of Solar Cooking equipment in Uganda, located in Buyembe, Mayuge district. We’re a team of young enthusiasts who will stop at nothing to spread the word about using the direct energy of the sun to prepare the local meals we love.

We’ve built this with real field work — demonstrations, home and group cooking parties, and steady growth across a full project year (May 2022–May 2023). If you’ve ever wanted a cleaner, smarter way to cook, you’re in the right place.
