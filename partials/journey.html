Our journey (the short version)

MESCL started as a bold idea: help communities cook safely while saving the environment — using the sun and smarter stoves. With seed support to stock the first appliances, we began doing what matters most: showing up on the ground.

During demonstrations and home or group cooking parties, something amazing happens: once people see the appliances working with real local meals, the conversation shifts from “Does it work?” to “How soon can I get one?” Those days are our favorite — learning, cooking, and sharing a happy meal at the end.

Along the way we kept learning too — networking and training in Kampala (March 2023), visits that inspired better community participation, and outreach trips as far as Moroto (NAPAK) to share skills and explore what a future branch could look like.

What’s next? We’re excited about making purchasing easier (like instalments), expanding with briquettes, and doing even more demos — because that’s where the biggest wins happen.
